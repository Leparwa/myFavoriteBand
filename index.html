About Wakadinali

Wakadinali group, which was formed in 2003, has three members including Scar, Domani and SewerSydaa. The group was founded by Scar and Domani, and Sewer Syder joined them later on. Among their best songs include Kovu, Kuna Siku Youths Wataungana, Lockdown, and Ndani ya Cockpit, just to mention but a few. The group has won several awards including Best Rap Group 2019, Best Album, Song and Collabo of the Year in 2019. Over the past two years, the group has been performing in several top shows and events. They also have a show called The Rym Gym which takes place on the last Sunday of each month in Umoja.
